The Trilogysystem of MTLD

Most launchpads allow "luck" to dispose of allocated tokens at launch. Unlike them, our launchpad is fair and equitable for all and yet rewards token holders. MetaLauchpad allows you to invest in innovative projects of the future without having MTLD tokens.

You just need to fill in the whitelist and wait for the results in our media resources!

The MetaLauchpad fundraising will follow the trilogy system marked in three rounds. The system ensures guaranteed fund allocation to the participants base.
We are one of the first platforms for launching crypto projects in the field of AR&VRs.

  • Round 1 100% of investors with staked MTLD tokens. All MTLD Token Stake Holders can contribute based on - MTLD tokens Staked - KYC - Whitelisting.
  • Round 2 Optional. 100% of investors with MTLD token holders. All MTLD Token Holders can contribute based on - MTLD tokens in their wallet irrespective of Staking - KYC - Whitelisting.
  • Round 3 Held among the participants Whitelist. The round is open to everyone. Whitelist - KYC.

DEFI solutions from MTLD

  • IDO Launchpad for Decentralized Fundraising MetaLauchpad will facilitate upcoming Crypto ecosystem projects to raise funds in decentralized manner. All the MTLD token staked users will get priority pass based on their staked amount.
  • Lottery Platform The platform lets users indulge in lottery and earn through their luck. The lottery smart contract shall select winners from the pool. It makes the lottery decentralized and fair.
  • Token Staking MetaLauchpad lets MTLD token users earn rewards if they stake MTLD tokens. The more they stake MTLD tokens, more the rewards they will have from the platform.
  • Token Vesting MetaLauchpad launchpad projects to lock the tokens. Teams, companies, and any user can lock their tokens that will be released on maturity dates through smart contract.
